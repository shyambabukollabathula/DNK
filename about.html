About D N K

Dakghar Niryat Kendra which means ‘export facilitation centre.’ The service aims to facilitate the small and medium business exporters, whereby with the help of the DNK software, its customers can file postal bill of exports online through the web link created for this purpose.

Parcel Delivery: E-commerce has seen exponential growth, and postal services play a pivotal role in delivering packages to customers' doorsteps. Companies rely on reliable postal services to meet customer expectations and grow their businesses.

International Trade: Cross-border e-commerce and international trade heavily depend on efficient post office delivery services for the transportation of goods and documents. These services facilitate global economic activities and contribute significantly to economic growth.

Remote Areas: Post office delivery services are often the only means of communication and commerce for people living in remote and underserved areas. They bridge the digital divide by providing access to essential services and information.

Financial Services: In many countries, post offices offer banking and financial services, making them a lifeline for individuals who lack access to traditional banks. Post offices promote financial inclusion and economic stability in marginalized communities.

Online Tracking: Customers can track the progress of their parcels in real-time, increasing transparency and reliability.

Eco-Friendly Initiatives: Many postal services are adopting eco-friendly practices, such as using electric vehicles and reducing carbon emissions in their operations.

Digital Integration: Postal services have integrated with e-commerce platforms, offering seamless online shopping experiences with reliable delivery options.
